About Us!

Code.Brew is a food and beverage concept designed specifically for corporate and office environments. We provide convenient, high-quality meal options and a dedicated coffee and drinks bar for employees. Our operations focus on efficiency, consistency, and accessibility, making us suitable for offices with limited food preparation areas while still delivering satisfying, well-prepared meals. Code.Brew's first operational branch is currently located at Ingram Micro, McKinley, Taguig City, where we serve employees daily through an office-friendly service model.
